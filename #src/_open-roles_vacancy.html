Customer Service / Sales Representative

We are excited to bring an outgoing individual with prior customer service and sales experience onto our team. The duties of the Customer Service/Sales Representative include, but are not limited to.

Who you are

  • You are someone who thrives under pressure – you know how to stay calm and collected in difficult situations
  • You are able to switch from high-level to low-level. You trust your team to make decisions but can step in when needed
  • You are a communicator – you give clear instructions and guidance and provide great feedback
  • You naturally see opportunities to upsell more value to your customers
  • You worked at a high-growth organisation before, preferably in CPM, ERP, SaaS, FinTech or Professional Services

What you will do

  • Build and scale Professional Services and Customer Success following best practices
  • Lead client implementation service delivery providing management and oversight of projects and staffing, including project assignments, capacity planning and profitability
  • Implement global services processes, methodology and tools to ensure consistency, quality, reliability and repeatability of projects delivery
  • Develop customer outreach cadence for Customer Success team, building and scaling Customer Support process, structure Post-Implementation process
  • Source, hire and retain top talent for your teams. Proactively coach team members through regular 1:1s
  • Manage escalated issues and facilitate resolution with the team and wider business, proposing solutions and recommendations to clients
  • Work cross-functionally with Engineering, Operations, Product and Senior Management to effectively guide projects as and when required
  • Oversee operational and financial metrics, including time-to-implement, customer satisfaction, revenue, gross margin and utilization
  • Collaborate with Sales and prospects to scope high-level requirements during the sales cycle

What You Need

  • You have a Bachelor degree or equivalent work experience with a financial background – you can read and interpret financial statements easily
  • You have previous people management experience – you know how to manage resourcing, coaching and feedback
  • You have previous client management experience – you know how to manage expectations, negotiate and deal with escalations
  • You have previous project management experience – you know how to manage timelines, budgets and metrics

What We Offer

  • Ample room for growth as you contribute to the company’s success
  • Competitive salary with option to receive company shares
  • A remote-first working environment with access to global top talent
  • You can start right away!
