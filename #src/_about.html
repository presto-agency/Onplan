OnPlan is the solution for growing organizations that don’t have the time or resources for legacy planning tools. It’s a intuitive platform and service. OnPlan is a modular financial platform that helps businesses to perform budgeting, forecasting, KPI tracking, dashboarding, benchmarking and more.

David Greenbaum

OnPlan Founder & CEO

How it works

OnPlan is a financial forecasting platform built on Google Sheets. We make financial modelling beautiful through tailored financial models, visualizations, integrated workflows and native scenario and variance functionality. OnPlan improves on traditional spreadsheet financial models with data integrations to ERP and CRM software and extensibility that is inherent in a modular modelling platform.

Customers can get started with OnPlan’s quick start program where we upload and convert your existing Excel model into our platform. Anything that can be modelled in Excel can be uploaded into the platform and integrated into an existing model.

For who

OnPlan is an ideal solution for companies experiencing spreadsheet pain, but not yet ready to adopt the complicated workflows of heavier forecasting tools — or their high fees.

The platform provides better visibility into a company's financial and operational dynamics Insightful visualizations that can drive improvements in decision making leading to increased profitability and cash flow
