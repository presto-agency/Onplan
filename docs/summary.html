Use your data for better decisions

Static planning tools are not flexible enough to deal with the rapid change in business today. For a business to thrive, its tools need to evolve with it, providing real time insights to drive future growth

Budgeting & forecasting

Best practice budgeting and forecasting involve organization-wide collaboration and elimination of manual input and validation. With OnPlan's permissions and integrations, your whole team can collaborate while eliminating manual inputs.

Variance analysis

Budget vs actual analysis can point you to operational issues to correct and provide better insight on business drivers. With OnPlan, you can analyze variances at the click of a button, giving real time insight and facilitating quicker decision making.

Scenario analysis

You operate in an uncertain world; quickly assessing multiple options can help you make decisions with greater confidence. With OnPlan, you can create new scenarios in seconds and compare them with your base case. If your model changes, your scenarios update automatically.

Tailored reporting

Provide the right views to every stakeholder so everyone can collaborate and move the business forward together. With OnPlan, you can tailor dashboards by team or person, so that everyone has access to the real-time information they need to make better decisions.
