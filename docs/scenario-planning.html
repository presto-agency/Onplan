Plan for the Unpredictable

OnPlan’s streamlined scenario planning tools make it easy to game out many different options — and choose the right path.

Book a Demo

Unified or independent

Run new scenarios in seconds and adjust them on the fly.

Scenarios can be set up at the click of a button; structural changes to your base model can be changed one scenario at a time or synced across all of them.

Variance analysis with a click

Compare the consequences of every scenario easily.

Side-by-side comparisons are a snap, between budget and actuals or between scenarios

Get the insights you need to make the right call.

Collaboration

Get the right insights from across the business by bringing stakeholders into the scenario planning process with collaboration and permissions so everyone can contribute without oversharing sensitive info.

All your data, up to date

OnPlan’s integrations ensure your stakeholders are using the same data set, and can spend their time planning for scenario outcomes, rather than reconciling data differences.
