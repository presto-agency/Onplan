Quickbooks

Quickbooks is a cloud based accounting software package targeted at small and medium sized businesses.

Our integration

OnPlan has a direct integration with Quickbooks ensuring you are always working with up to date historical financial information on the OnPlan platform. In addition, the integration also allows you to drill into historical transactional data without having to leave the OnPlan platform.
