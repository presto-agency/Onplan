About Redbubble

Redbubble is a marketplace for independent artists to sell their work, headquartered in Australia and with offices in San Francisco and Berlin. It offers a print-on-demand service for independent artists to design and sell products. Artists can include their designs on a wide range of products, including t-shirts, mugs, and coasters. The Australian stock exchange listed organization generates over A$400m in annual revenue.

Spreadsheet pain

Before implementing OnPlan, Redbubble used a series of google sheets for planning and analysis. Historical financial information for its five legal entities would be copied and pasted manually out of NetSuite and into various google sheets, which would then update a master google sheet used for forecasting and reporting. Key HR information from various payroll software packages would also be updated manually.

The FP&A team wanted to improve its processes by removing the manual elements of the processes to free up more time for analysis. It also wanted to increase the engagement of non finance staff by involving them more directly in the budgeting process and by giving them live access to relevant financial information.
